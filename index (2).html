Our Story

Built From
Love & Purpose

Moses and Philomena Mensah came from humble beginnings in Ghana. After years of living and working in the United Kingdom, they made a bold and courageous decision — to return home and build something that would change lives forever.

In 2006, using their personal savings and a bank loan, they purchased land in Takoradi and constructed a school from the ground up. Modelled on St Mark's Academy in the UK, Oakhill Royal was born with one powerful mission: to give children the opportunities they never had.

"We wanted to provide children with opportunities that we didn't have — giving them a good start in life."

— Moses Mensah, Founder & Headmaster
